Cookie Policy

How we use cookies on babinom.com.

This Cookie Policy applies to the babinom.com website. It does not apply to the Babinom mobile app, which does not use cookies but uses similar technologies described in the App Privacy Policy.

Effective date: June 11, 2026 · Last updated: June 11, 2026

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They let the website remember information about your visit, like your preferred language or login state. We also describe similar technologies — local storage, session storage, pixels, beacons — here because they serve the same purposes from a regulatory standpoint.

Strictly necessary (always on)

These cookies are required for the site to function and cannot be disabled. They do not store personal identifiers beyond what's needed for the requested service.

session

Maintains your browsing session. Duration: browser session.

consent

Records your cookie-preference choice. Duration: 12 months.

csrf

Prevents cross-site request forgery on form submissions. Duration: browser session.

Analytics (opt-in)

We use PostHog to understand how visitors find and use the site so we can improve it. Analytics cookies are only set after you give consent via the cookie banner. PostHog cookies (prefix ph_*) handle session and event tracking with a 12-month duration. PostHog is configured for EU data residency. We do not pass cookie data to advertising networks.

Marketing (currently not used)

We do not currently use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or third-party marketing. If this changes we will update this Policy and ask for your consent again.

Your choices

You can manage cookies in three places: the cookie banner when you first visit the site (accept all, necessary only, or per-category), your browser settings (most browsers let you block or delete cookies), or by clicking the “Cookie Preferences” link in our site footer at any time. Blocking strictly-necessary cookies may break parts of the site such as forms or login.

Third parties

The site may contain links to the App Store and Google Play. Those services set their own cookies under their own policies. We do not control them.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy when our use of cookies changes. We will note the change in the cookie banner if your prior consent no longer covers the new uses.
