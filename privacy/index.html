Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Babinom (“Babinom,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Babinom mobile app and website. Babinom is a baby name app for couples.

Information you provide

We may collect information you enter in the app, such as your account details, app preferences, selected countries or languages, liked names, rejected names, matched names, custom names, room codes, and support messages.

Purchase information

Purchases are processed by Apple or Google. We do not collect or store your full payment card details. Purchase identifier. A RevenueCat app user ID used solely to manage your purchase entitlement.

Technical information

We may receive basic technical information needed to keep the app working, such as app version, device type, operating system, crash details, and diagnostic logs.

How we use information

We use information to provide the app, sync partner rooms, show name recommendations, save your preferences, process and restore Lifetime Premium access, respond to support requests, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.

Product interaction, such as swipes, matches, preferences, and feature usage, helps us provide app functionality and improve Babinom. This data may be linked to your account when needed to provide app functionality and improve the experience.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate the app, such as authentication, database hosting, purchase entitlement, and app infrastructure providers.

Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide Babinom, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain purchase entitlement records. You may contact us to request account or data deletion.

Children

Babinom is intended for parents and adults choosing baby names. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can update preferences in the app, restore purchases through the app, and contact us to request help with account access, data deletion, or privacy questions.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to keep Babinom safe and reliable.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and provide notice when appropriate.

Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact us at support@babinom.com.
